Aero Design Challenge

The purpose of the SAEISS Aero Design Challenge is to promote and develop Indian expertise and experience in unmanned systems technologies at the university and college levels. Aero Design Challenge competition is intended to conceive, design and develop a prototype of a fixed wing UAV, meeting the mission requirements.
